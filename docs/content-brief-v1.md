# Content Brief V1

Editorial direction for the first public version of the portfolio.

## Language

The site content is written in Rioplatense Spanish for V1. An English version can be added later.

## Positioning

Florencia Frumento is a Content Designer and UX Writer who brings a strong background in language, writing, and literary studies into digital products.

The core message:

```text
Transformo palabras en experiencias de producto claras, útiles y humanas.
```

## Audience

The site speaks mainly to recruiters and hiring teams, but in a subtle way. It should feel like a professional presentation, not like an urgent "open to work" page.

Secondary audiences:

- Product, UX, and content teams.
- Potential collaborators.
- People who want to understand Florencia's professional profile quickly.

## What People Should Remember

- Florencia is a Content Designer and UX Writer.
- She is Licenciada y Profesora en Letras from Universidad de Buenos Aires.
- Her differentiator is the intersection between language, writing, UX, and digital products.
- She sees words as a design tool: words can guide decisions, reduce friction, and make product experiences clearer and more human.
- She is interested in product thinking, data-informed decisions, and AI applied to digital product work.

## Tone

Professional, tech-oriented, close, human, and enthusiastic.

Avoid:

- Sounding desperate for work.
- Overexplaining the flower/sunflower concept.
- Making the portfolio feel like a generic template.

## Visual Concept Note

The sunflower idea can inform the visual direction later: warmth, light, clarity, orientation, growth, and movement toward positive environments.

Use it subtly. The portfolio should not be filled with literal flowers or poetic explanations.

```text
Lenguaje, producto y experiencia de usuario.
```

```text
Soy Content Designer y UX Writer, Licenciada y Profesora en Letras por la Universidad de Buenos Aires.

Mi formación viene del lenguaje, la literatura, la escritura y el análisis. Mi práctica profesional está en cruzar todo eso con productos digitales: entender qué necesita una persona en cada momento, ordenar información compleja y diseñar palabras que hagan que una tarea sea más simple, intuitiva y amable.

Creo en el poder enorme del lenguaje para construir mejores experiencias. Para mí, escribir no es decorar una interfaz: es diseñar caminos, decisiones y momentos de claridad.
```
